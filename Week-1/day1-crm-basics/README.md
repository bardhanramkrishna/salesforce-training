# 1. What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is a cloud-based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform used by businesses to manage customers, sales, support, marketing, and business operations.

It helps organizations:
- Store customer information
- Track customer interactions
- Automate business processes
- Improve customer relationships
- Increase productivity

---

# 2. What problem does Salesforce solve?

Salesforce solves problems related to customer management and business operations.

### Problems solved by Salesforce:
- Customer data stored in different places
- Difficulty tracking sales and support
- Poor communication with customers
- Manual and time-consuming business tasks
- Lack of business automation

Salesforce brings everything into one centralized platform.

---

# 3. What is CRM?

CRM stands for **Customer Relationship Management**.

CRM is a technology used to manage customer interactions, business relationships, sales, and support services.

### Benefits of CRM:
- Better customer communication
- Improved customer satisfaction
- Easy data management
- Faster support services
- Better sales tracking

---

# 4. What is an Object in Salesforce?

An Object in Salesforce is similar to a database table that stores information.

### Examples of Salesforce Objects:
| Object | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| Account | Stores company/customer details |
| Contact | Stores customer contact information |
| Lead | Stores potential customer details |

---

# 5. Difference Between Salesforce Admin and Developer

| Salesforce Admin | Salesforce Developer |
|------------------|----------------------|
| Manages Salesforce settings and users | Builds and customizes applications |
| Uses clicks and configuration | Uses coding and development |
| Creates reports and dashboards | Writes Apex and Lightning code |
| Handles security and permissions | Develops custom features |

---

# 6. What Does a Salesforce Developer Do?

A Salesforce Developer develops and customizes applications on the Salesforce platform.

### Responsibilities:
- Writing Apex code
- Creating Lightning Web Components
- Automating business processes
- Integrating external systems
- Building custom applications

---

# 7. Basic Salesforce Environment (Trailhead Playground)

Trailhead Playground is a practice environment provided by Salesforce.

### Uses of Trailhead Playground:
- Practice Salesforce concepts
- Complete hands-on challenges
- Build and test applications
- Learn safely without affecting real business data

---

# 8. Real-World Application Using Salesforce

## Hospital Management System

A Hospital Management System can be built using Salesforce.

### Features:
- Patient record management
- Appointment scheduling
- Doctor and staff management
- Automated notifications
- Customer support system
